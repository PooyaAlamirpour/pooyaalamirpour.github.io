About Me

I am a graduate student at the International Institute of Information Technology, Bangalore.

My on-going masters thesis investigates the synthesis of 3D worlds from monocular images. My work looks into how deep learning can be used with graphics to mimic urban scenes. My research interests revolve around integrating graphics with deep learning, data driven animation, augemented and virtual reality, and using graphics in health care and education.

I love to play the piano and illustrate. I am an experienced and at the same time amateur graphic designer. I’ve worked with several organizations and individuals to design logos, album art, brochures and templates. I spend most of my weekends baking for family and friends. I love indie music, a good paperback and going to the cinema.
